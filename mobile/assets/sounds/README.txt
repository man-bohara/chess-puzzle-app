Drop two short audio clips here (mp3 or wav, < 100 KB each):

  wrong.mp3   — short error / buzz, plays on a wrong move
  solved.mp3  — celebratory chime, plays when the puzzle is solved

The SoundService loads these from `assets/sounds/`. Missing files are
swallowed silently, so the app keeps working until you add real audio.

Good free sources:
  - freesound.org (filter by CC0 license)
  - mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/game/
  - pixabay.com/sound-effects/
